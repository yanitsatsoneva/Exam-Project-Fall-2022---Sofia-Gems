National Theatre Garden

5 Dyakon Ignatiy Str.

In front of Bulgaria's National Theatre "Ivan Vazov" there is a vast garden with many benches, lawns and a fountain providing coolness in the summer days. During the day it is the perfect place for a walk or short chill stay with friends accompanied by street jazz musicians. At night though, the garden turns into a meeting spot for hundreds of young people who have fun way into the night.
